[[LG-5-1]]
==== LG 5-1: Use AI for architecture discovery and system understanding

Participants know how AI can aid the understanding of existing systems by 

* summarizing and analyzing available system artifacts
* digesting and analyzing source code and deployment artifacts
* developing live system documentation like diagrams of structure and flow 
* extracting conceptual information and linking it to known patterns, styles
* extracting technology information
* architectural characteristics

Participants understand the strengths and limitations of AI-supported architecture discovery, and know how to validate findings with inspection, communication and the help of traditional static/dynamic analysis tools

[[LG-5-2]]
==== LG 5-2: Use AI to interpret and synthesize structural information

Participants understand how AI can support the interpretation of structural information from different technical artifacts and sources. They know how partial findings can be combined into coherent architectural views and explanations.

* Interpret structural information from code, repositories, interfaces, and dependency information 
* Combine findings from different sources into a usable representation (like knowledge graphs, structure models etc.)
* Identify gaps, inconsistencies, and uncertainties in reconstructed architecture information

[[LG-5-3]]
==== LG 5-3: Support technology detection and pattern recognition with AI

Participants know how AI can be used to detect technologies, frameworks, integration styles, and recurring solution patterns in existing systems. They understand how such findings can contribute to architecture recovery and modernization work.

* Identify relevant technologies and frameworks in existing systems e.g. by using code property graphs (Joern)
* Recognize recurring architectural and design patterns 
* Relate detected technologies and patterns to architectural structures and possible modernization concerns

[[LG-5-4]]
==== LG 5-4: Use AI for business rule extraction

Participants understand how AI can support the identification and documentation of business rules from existing artifacts. They know how AI can help uncover implicit domain logic and prepare it for communication, validation, and further architectural work.

* Extract candidate business rules from code, tests, configuration, and related artifacts 
* Relate extracted rules to domain concepts and business processes 
* Prepare extracted rules for validation with domain experts and stakeholders

[[LG-5-5]]
==== LG 5-5: Derive architecture documentation from development artifacts

Participants know how AI can support the creation of architecture documentation from live development artifacts. They understand how AI can help transform technical information into structured architectural descriptions for further use in analysis, communication, and maintenance.

* Generate architecture-related documentation from code, repositories, interfaces, and other development artifacts 
* Transform technical findings into understandable architectural descriptions and use templates (like arc42, architecture vision documents or canvases) 
* Understand the need to validate generated documentation against the actual system

[[LG-5-6]]
==== LG 5-6: Reconstruct and maintain up-to-date system views

Participants understand how AI can support the reconstruction and ongoing maintenance of architectural views for existing systems. They know how current system views can be derived and updated from changing implementation artifacts.

* Reconstruct relevant system views from available technical information 
* Maintain architectural views in alignment with system evolution and understand the value of text based diagram construction (e.g. with Mermaid, Structurizr)
* Understand the value of current system views for analysis, communication, and modernization

[[LG-5-7]]
==== LG 5-7: Support the creation and maintenance of Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) with GenAI

Participants know how AI can support the creation, refinement, and maintenance of Architecture Decision Records. They understand how AI can help relate decisions to context, alternatives, rationale, and consequences without replacing architectural accountability.

* Prepare ADR drafts from available project and system artifacts 
* Support the documentation of context, decision options, rationale, and consequences 
* Maintain ADRs in alignment with evolving systems and decisions by flagging existing ADRs, proposing updates or monitoring certain technologies or development artifacts.
